About Me

Welcome to my blog! I am a student at Lindenwood University, and this site is dedicated to my work in ENGL 170: First-Year Writing for the Spring 2026 semester.

In this course, we are exploring the relationship between AI, writing, and intellectual work. This blog will serve as a digital journal where I post three times a week to reflect on these topics and engage with the ideas of my classmates.

My goal for this semester is to better understand how technology influences the way we think and communicate.
